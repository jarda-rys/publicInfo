Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Najdi Lékárnu is designed to help users easily locate pharmacies in the Czech Republic. We respect your privacy and do not process any personal user data.

2. Data Sources

The app uses open data provided by the State Institute for Drug Control (SÚKL). These datasets are publicly available under the terms listed here.

3. Device Location

The app may use your device's location to display nearby pharmacies. Your location is not stored or transmitted to any server.

4. Advertising and Analytics

This version of the app does not include any advertising systems or third-party analytics tools.

5. Data Sharing

The app does not share any data with third parties. All displayed information comes from public sources.

6. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be communicated through app updates.
